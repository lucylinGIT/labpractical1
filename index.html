Waterfalls of Hamilton, Ontario

Hamilton is home to over 100 waterfalls. It is easy to fell overwhelmed by your options. Which falls should you visit? What is the best way to get there?

The following maps highlight the most popular waterfalls as well as the closest entry points to make your next trip to Hamilton easier.
